Why These Topics Matter to Me

A topic that I am currently interested in is oil markets and how the price of oil changes. While writing this blurb, the US - Iraq conflict is ongoing, creating a lot of stress in the oil market due to the blockade of the Strait of Hormuz. Not only does this event impact WTI and Brent Oil prices, but it also drives industries up, down, and sideways. For example, Airline stocks have been getting killed recently. I believe that understanding how markets hold exposure is key to creating a portfolio that yields high returns.

I am also super interested in Data Viz. I believe that with proper skills in this department, one can get a point across in an instant. Especially since I am interested in the world of finance, gaining high exposure to Data Viz methodologies will prove highly beneficial for my career. I have completed many projects in this space, some of which include predicting recessions, looking at ETF movements, and even looking at crime rates in major cities.

In the future, I hope to keep building skills in these areas so I can work on challenging problems and meaningful opportunities. I want to improve not only my technical abilities, but also my ability to explain ideas clearly and think critically about strategy. That is one reason this topic matters to me so much. It is not only about information or formulas, but about using knowledge to make better decisions and create real impact over time.
