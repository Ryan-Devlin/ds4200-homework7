About Me

My name is Ryan Devlin, and I am a student at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Business. I am interested in the way data, technology, and business strategy come together to solve real-world problems. One of the reasons I enjoy studying data science is that it gives me tools to understand patterns, make better decisions, and communicate insights clearly. At the same time, my interest in business helps me think about how those insights can create value in companies, markets, and everyday life.

Outside of school, I enjoy working on projects that connect analytics with entrepreneurship. I like exploring ideas related to finance, investing, startups, and new technology. I am especially interested in how innovative businesses grow, how investors evaluate opportunities, and how data can be used to support strategic decisions. I also enjoy building things, whether that means creating presentations, analyzing datasets, or designing project ideas that could become real products or businesses in the future.

This website highlights some of my interests and gives a better picture of what I enjoy learning about. I wanted to create a simple site that introduces who I am while also sharing a few topics I care about. In the future, this website could grow into a personal portfolio where I share more of my work, projects, and experiences. Overall, I see this as a useful way to practice web development while also creating something personal and meaningful that reflects my academic interests and long-term goals.
