About Me

My name is Ryan Devlin, and I am a student at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Business. I am interested in the way technology, analytics, and business strategy work together to solve real-world problems. One reason I enjoy data science is that it helps turn raw information into useful insights. Learning how to organize data, visualize patterns, and communicate conclusions clearly is valuable in many different settings. At the same time, studying business helps me understand how decisions are made inside organizations and how companies use information to improve performance and create value.

I am also interested in entrepreneurship, investing, and innovation. I like thinking about how new products are created, how businesses grow, and how ideas can move from concept to reality. These interests connect closely with my academic work because they require both creativity and analytical thinking. Whether I am looking at a dataset, planning a project, or learning about markets, I enjoy trying to understand the bigger picture behind the numbers. I think it is important not only to know how to calculate results, but also to know what those results mean and how they can support better decisions.

This website gives a simple introduction to who I am and what I care about. I wanted to make a website that feels organized, professional, and easy to navigate while also reflecting my interests. In the future, I could expand this site into a larger portfolio that includes projects, coursework, and other experiences. For now, it serves as both a personal introduction and a way to practice building and styling a website using HTML and CSS.
